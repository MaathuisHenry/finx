Welcome to the FIN-X Project


The FIN-X project is focused on developing tools to enhance explainability for AI systems in the financial sector. This project aims to foster trust by enabling internal users, such as risk underwriters and claims handlers, to better understand and explain AI-driven decisions.


Our Mission


Our mission is to create practical, user-friendly explainability tools that support employees in the financial sector, enabling them to confidently communicate AI outcomes to customers, fostering transparency and trust in AI technology.
