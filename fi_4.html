Feature Importance

This prototype visualizes the top four most important factors that contributed to the predicted risk of car insurance fraud. It focuses on risk-increasing features only. Positive or mitigating factors are not shown here but may be included in future use cases depending on the design intent.


Evaluation Summary

This prototype stood out as the best performing design in the feature importance category. It was rated highly by users.


No further design changes were made, as the initial version already aligned well with user expectations and model communication goals.
