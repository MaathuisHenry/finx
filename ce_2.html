Contrastive / Similar Examples

This prototype shows a new insurance claim being assessed by comparing it to similar past cases—some that were previously labeled fraudulent, and others that were not. The goal is to provide users with relatable examples to help them interpret the current risk assessment.


Evaluated Prototype – User Feedback

Users found it difficult to understand where to begin visually, which made the information harder to digest. The purpose of the wavy shape in the middle of the screen was unclear, and the positioning of the main case outside the bounding box (with a white background) caused confusion.


Improved Version – Design Iteration

  • The layout was restructured to follow a more familiar left-to-right, top-to-bottom reading flow, helping users grasp the comparison process more naturally.
  • The design now visually aligns related elements, making the information easier to scan and compare at a glance.
