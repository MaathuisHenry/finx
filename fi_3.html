Feature Importance

This prototype highlights the top four most important factors contributing to the predicted risk of car insurance fraud. These are risk-increasing factors only. Mitigating (positive) factors are not included here but may be explored in future versions depending on context and goals.


Evaluated Prototype – User Feedback

The visual use of circles was intended to represent the relative strength or “heaviness” of each factor. However, users found this representation unclear and not intuitive.


Improved Version – Design Iteration

  • The circles were made solid rather than outlined, making their relative size and weight easier to perceive at a glance.
  • A color gradient was introduced to visually reinforce impact with the aim to help users intuitively assess the relative strength of each feature.
