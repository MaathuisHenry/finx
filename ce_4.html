Contrastive / Similar Examples

This prototype shows a current insurance claim compared with previous cases that are either similar and flagged as fraud, or similar and not flagged. The aim is to support assessors with concrete, contrastive examples that provide broader context for decision-making.


Initial Prototype – User Feedback

Users noted that the label and positioning of “Case A: Current case” created visual noise and disrupted the logical flow of information. It wasn’t clear where attention should be directed first.


Improved Version – Design Iteration

  • The information layout was revised to follow a natural western reading pattern—top-right to bottom-left—making the progression of comparisons easier to digest.
  • Content was aligned more clearly to visually link related elements and support rapid scanning and comparison.
  • A more neutral color palette was introduced to remove implicit bias and help users more easily identify which areas require focused attention.
