Feature Importance

This prototype visualizes the top four most important factors contributing to the risk of car insurance fraud, as determined by a predictive model. The visualization focuses on factors that increase the predicted risk. Factors that could mitigate or reduce the risk are not shown in this version, but may be relevant for alternative use cases or future iterations.


Evaluated Prototype – User Feedback

The initial version presented the key contributing factors, but some users found the scale ambiguous and requested a more intuitive, generalized depiction.


Improved Version – Design Iteration

  • The vertical axis labels were updated from "0" and "50" to "Low" and "High" to improve scale interpretability and make values like 41, 34, and 12 more meaningful.
  • The "Show all factors" button was repositioned directly beneath the initial list of visible features, strengthening the perceived relationship and clarifying that additional factors exist.
  • The graph legend was revised to include more explanatory text, enhancing clarity and general understanding.
