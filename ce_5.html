Contrastive / Similar Examples

This prototype supports users by showing a claim under review alongside previous cases that share similar characteristics. These contrastive examples include both claims flagged as fraudulent and those not flagged, helping users assess risk based on historical context.


Evaluation Summary

This design was evaluated positively by users. The layout, context, and information presentation were found clear and useful.


No visual or structural changes were made, as the prototype met the intended goals effectively. However, some users indicated that including detailed feature importances alongside the similar cases could lead to cognitive overload. This feedback highlights the need for careful balance between transparency and clarity when combining multiple explanation types.
