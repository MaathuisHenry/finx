Funding and Subsidy Information


The FIN-X project is supported through funding from the Nationaal Regieorgaan Praktijkgericht Onderzoek SIA, as part of the RAAK-mkb subsidy scheme. This scheme is designed to foster collaboration between research institutions and industry partners, particularly in applied research that can have a practical impact within sectors like finance.


Subsidy Details


  • Subsidy Provider: Nationaal Regieorgaan Praktijkgericht Onderzoek SIA
  • Subsidy Scheme: RAAK-mkb
  • Dossier Number: RAAK.MKB17.003
  • Funding Period: September 2022

About the RAAK-mkb Scheme


The RAAK-mkb scheme encourages collaboration between educational institutions, industry organizations, and SMEs, supporting research projects that yield practical tools and insights. Through this project, FIN-X aims to create meaningful explainability tools that benefit both internal users in the financial sector and the broader community, promoting trust and transparency in AI-driven decisions.


Project Partners


This project brings together a diverse group of partners, including higher education institutions, industry leaders, and associations. Together, we are developing tools that translate complex AI outcomes into understandable insights, equipping internal users to communicate transparently with customers.
