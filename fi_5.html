Feature Importance

This prototype presents the top four most important features contributing to the predicted risk of car insurance fraud. These factors are those increasing the risk. Mitigating (positive) factors are not displayed, but could be useful for other use cases where a balanced explanation is preferred.


Evaluated Prototype – User Feedback

While the design aimed to visually distinguish risk factors using color, users found the meaning unclear—particularly the color usage in boxes and the styling of the “Show all factors” button.


Improved Version – Design Iteration

  • To improve user comprehension, the layout was refined and the box that had no corresponding value was visually neutralized by removing its color.
  • A legend was added to clearly explain the meaning of colors used in the feature boxes, supporting a stronger mental model for users.
  • The “Show all factors” button was also restyled to better communicate its function and avoid confusion with informational color coding.
